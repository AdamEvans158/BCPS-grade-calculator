The BCPS Grading Scale

Baltimore County Public Schools or BCPS for short uses a standard 4.0 grading scale. But they also have a weighted scale with Honors, GT, AP, IB, and Duel Enrollment considerations. The weighted GPA is also called QPA (Quality Point Average), is on a 6.0 scale with GT, AP, IB, and Duel Enrollment courses being worth 6 and honors courses being worth 5.

How to Calculate GPA

To calculate your unweighted GPA, you need to the point values for each letter. An A = 4, B = 3, C = 2, D = 1, and E = 0. Note that number percentages are not considered. Only the letter, and there are no + or - grades. To calculuate your GPA add all the letter values for each of your classes and divide by the total number of classes. Or if you want your GPA in a single class, Add all the letter values for each grading period and final exam and divide by the amount of marking periods + 1.
